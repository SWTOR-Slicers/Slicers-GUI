Slicers GUI Settings

Accessibility

Alert Notifications:

Determines which log types create alert notifications. Setting applies to all app windows.

Display Tooltips:

Enables/disables the tooltips that are on most of the input field labels and buttons. (These do not include the tooltips that show the full path of file/folder input fields)

Path Tooltips:

Enables/disables the tooltips that are on most of the input fields. (These do not include the tooltips on field labels and buttons)

Audio

Ambient Background Music:

Enables/disables ambient music that is played in the background of the app. If enabled you also have the option to set your own music file(s).

Play When Minimized:

Whether or not the ambient music will play when the main app window is minimized.
